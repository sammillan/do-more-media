About Me

Dominic Hayles

Based in Hastings, East Sussex, I bring over 7 years of experience in professional videography and content creation. My expertise spans drone operation, camera work, color grading, and video editing.

From corporate events and product launches to movie production, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects that challenge and inspire me. My goal is simple: to bring your ideas to life in the most classy and professional way possible.

Whether you need stunning aerial footage, compelling brand content, or complete video production services, I'm here to help tell your story.
